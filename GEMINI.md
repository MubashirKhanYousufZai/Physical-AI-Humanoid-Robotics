## üìò Project Overview

The book, *Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics*, was created to synthesize complex technical topics into an accessible format for students and enthusiasts.

**Key Deliverables:**

*   **Five Core Chapters:** Covering Foundations of Physical AI, Robot Architecture, Perception Systems, Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), and Control Systems.

*   **AI-Powered RAG Chatbot:** An interactive chatbot providing answers directly from the book's content, significantly enhanced for reliability and user experience.
* **Structured Content:** Utilizing professional Markdown (`.md` format) for clean structure and easy digital publishing.
* **Educational Objective:** To inspire the next generation to explore and contribute to the field of embodied intelligent machines.

## üß† Technical Focus

The project chapters specifically explored the integration of modern AI concepts (like **Reinforcement Learning** and **NLP**) with the mechanical and control challenges unique to humanoid robots (like achieving **dynamic stability** and high **Degrees of Freedom**). **The integration of a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot further exemplifies practical AI application, allowing users to interact directly with the book's content.**
